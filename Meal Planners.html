MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular meal planning app that helps users track their caloric intake, macronutrient consumption, and exercise routine. The app has a large database of foods and recipes, making it easy for users to log their meals and monitor their progress towards their fitness goals. MyFitnessPal also offers personalized meal plans and recipes based on user preferences and dietary restrictions.

Cookpad

Cookpad is a meal planning app that offers a community-driven platform for sharing and discovering recipes. The app allows users to browse through a large collection of user-generated recipes, save their favorites, and create personalized cookbooks. Cookpad also offers a meal planning feature that allows users to schedule their meals for the week and generate a grocery list based on the selected recipes. The app also has a feature that allows users to share their own recipes and connect with other home cooks.

Eat This Much

Eat This Much is a meal planning app that generates personalized meal plans based on user preferences and dietary requirements. The app allows users to set their daily calorie and macronutrient goals, and generates meal plans with recipes and grocery lists. Eat This Much also allows users to customize their meal plans by selecting specific foods or excluding certain ingredients.
