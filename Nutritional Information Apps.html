MyPlate Calorie Counter

MyPlate Calorie Counter is a popular mobile application designed to help users track their daily calorie intake, monitor their weight, and improve their overall health and fitness. The app is developed by Livestrong.com and is available for both Android and iOS devices.MyPlate Calorie Counter also offers a variety of additional features such as the ability to track water intake, monitor exercise, set reminders, and connect with friends for motivation and support. The app also offers premium features such as customized meal plans and recipes, and access to a team of registered dietitians for personalized advice and support.

Ate Food Journal

Ate Food Journal is a mobile application designed to help people keep track of their daily food intake. It is available for both iOS and Android devices.The app allows users to take pictures of their meals and record what they ate, as well as the time and location of the meal. It also provides a space for users to write notes about their meals, such as how they felt while eating or any reactions they had to certain foods.Ate Food Journal emphasizes mindful eating, which means paying attention to the experience of eating, being present in the moment, and listening to the body's hunger and fullness signals. Ate Food Journal can be a helpful tool for anyone who wants to become more mindful of their eating habits and make healthier choices

MyNetDiary

MyNetDiary is a popular mobile application and website designed to help individuals track their diet, exercise, and weight loss progress. It is primarily used for weight management and is intended to help users make informed decisions about their daily food intake and physical activity.The application allows users to set goals for weight loss or maintenance, track their daily caloric intake, and log their exercise routines. It provides a database of thousands of foods and exercises, allowing users to search for specific items or scan the barcode of packaged foods to easily log their calorie and nutrient intake.MyNetDiary is a comprehensive tool for weight management and can be helpful for individuals looking to improve their diet and exercise habits.
